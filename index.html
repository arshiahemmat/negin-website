About Negin

Negin Khodami is a passionate English teacher dedicated to inspiring her students to achieve their linguistic goals. With an IELTS band score of 8 [1], she demonstrates a very good command of English, meaning she has a fully operational command of the language with only occasional unsystematic inaccuracies.

Having taught more than 20 students, Negin has guided learners from diverse backgrounds to success in language tests and everyday communication. She believes in creating a supportive and engaging learning environment where students can thrive.
